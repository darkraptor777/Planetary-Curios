[h1]Archaeological Dig Sites[/h1]
[b]How they work:[/b]
This mod adds new dig sites like those included in the Ancient Relics dlc (NOTE: the dlc is [b]NOT REQUIRED[/b] to be able to use these new dig sites). Rewards vary from relics, to techs and unique solar systems.
